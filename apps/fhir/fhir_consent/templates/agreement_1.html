Consent Agreement

Medicare Beneficiary Agreement to share information with Third Party Application

User agrees to share their Medicare Claims information with a 3PA. The agreement is activated by the User authorizing the 3PA via the "Authorization" screen.

Consent is granted for a limited period of time, as defined in the authorization screen.

Consent is granted to a limited range of the user's data as defined by the scopes selected in the authorization screen.

Consent is limited to the specific 3PA identified in the authorization screen and is not transferable.

Definitions

User: A Medicare Beneficiary with a MyMedicare.gov account.

Third Party Application (3PA): An application, program or mobile "app" that is registered on the CMS Blue Button API system.
