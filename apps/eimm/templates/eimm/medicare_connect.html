In this next step we will link your Medicare account to the CMS Blue Button API.

By clicking "Connect" you are agreeing to allow the CMS Blue Button API to connect to your MyMedicare.gov account to perform a one-time download of your Blue Button file. This is necessary so that we can identify your records in our new Blue Button system.
The process will take a few minutes to download your Medicare Blue Button file and analyze it to find your claims data.
it will then use the claims numbers to find matching claims in the CMS Blue Button API.
Once a link is made you will be able to connect health applications and research trials to your health data quickly and easily.

If you do not want to connect with MyMedicare.gov please select the Cancel button.
