It's Your Data

Taking Your Data with you

The CMS Advanced BlueButton Service makes it easy for you to control the applications, services or research programs that can connect to your claims information.
It is important to realize that, just like handing your downloaded BlueButton text file to someone else, once you have allowed an application to connect to your information they have a copy.
Before you connect an application to your MyMedicare information always make sure that you trust the application to take good care of your information.
But remember, if you change your mind you can come back to your My Account dashboard on MyMedicare.gov and disconnect the applications you want to stop using. When you update your connected applications settings the disconnection immediately stops any new data from flowing to the application you disconnected.
