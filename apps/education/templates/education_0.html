A New Generation of BlueButton

Put your health information to work for you or donate it to research

MyMedicare.gov introduces a new, advanced BlueButton service. It complements the existing BlueButton Text and PDF file download options.
The new service is a programmable interface. It enables organizations to create web and mobile applications that help you manage your health information. This lets you choose which applications you want to share your Medicare claims data with.
Once you choose to share with an application it does all the hard work of downloading and understanding your data. It can do this automatically on your behalf so that your information is ready prepared for you when you open the app.
This advanced interface lets you share information without having to share your password. Each application or service you connect is given a unique key that you control. You can delete their key at any time. Just login to MyMedicare.gov, access the "Connected Apps" panel in "My Account" and remove their key.
