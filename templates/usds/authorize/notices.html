The Transmitted Copy is Under Someone Else's Control

Once you approve sharing of your personally identifiable health information, a copy of your data will be transmitted to your selected third party. Whether or not your data appears on your device, that third party has control over your transmitted data. Whatever control you may have over your transmitted data must pass through your selected third party.

Once a copy is successfully transmitted to your designated third party, CMS and CMS Blue Button have no control over and are NOT RESPONIBLE for any risks to, or incidents involving that copy.

The Copy or Copies you Authorize Us to Transmit May Not Be Safe After Transmission.

You can use your MyMedicare.gov application dashboard to revoke access to any new information but this does not prevent the third party from using information you previously gave them permission to receive.
